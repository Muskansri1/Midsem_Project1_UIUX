Bringing Your Community to YourFingertips
"Design a mobile app that connects people with local businesses, clubs and group and encourages them to engage more with their community"
Problem Statement

Many people in our community struggle to discover and connect with local businesses, clubs, and groups that align with their interests and preferences. They often feel disconnected from the vibrant community around them and miss out on valuable opportunities for social interaction, support, and personal growth. Additionally, local businesses and community organizations may find it challenging to reach and engage with potential customers and members effectively.

Design Process

Design Process Explained

Empathize
Brainstroming Questions
Empathize is the initial phase of the design process where designers seek to understand the users, their needs, and their pain points. It involves putting yourself in the shoes of the users to gain a deep understanding of their experiences, emotions, and challenges.

Goal: To develop a profound sense of empathy for the end users, enabling designers to design solutions that truly cater to their needs.

Design
Hand Sketches, Wireframe, Prototype
The design phase is where designers turn the chosen concepts into tangible, visually appealing interfaces. It involves creating wireframes, prototypes, and mockups that reflect the final design. This phase ensures the design is user-centric and meets the identified needs.

Goal: To produce a polished and user-friendly design that aligns with the user requirements and concepts generated during the ideation phase.
Test
Usability Testing
The testing phase involves assessing the designed prototypes or interfaces with actual users. It helps in identifying usability issues, getting user feedback, and refining the design based on user responses.

Goal: To evaluate the design's effectiveness, identify areas for improvement, and ensure that the final product aligns with user expectations and needs.
